> *"The best architectures, requirements, and designs emerge from self-organizing teams"*

Software cannot realistically be planned entirely up front. Agile aknowledges this reality, and calls for projects to have momentum and an overal *direction*, but agile does not work well within the confines of a concrete imutable plan ([Adkins, 2010][1], [Eckstein, 2009][2], [Larman, 2004][3]).
