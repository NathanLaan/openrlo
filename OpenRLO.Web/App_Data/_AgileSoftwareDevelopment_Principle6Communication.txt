> *"The most efficient and effective method of conveying information to and within a development team is face-to-face conversation"*

Communication is key. This should be obvious. However, *passive communication* is not enough. Team members must be able to meet and communicate face-to-face ([Augustine, 2005][1], [Larman, 2004][2]).
