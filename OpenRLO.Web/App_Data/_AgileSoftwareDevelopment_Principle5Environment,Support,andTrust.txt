> *"Build projects around motivated individuals. Give them the environment and support they need, and trust them to get the job done"*

* The team must be supported, trusted, and empowered.
* This carries down to project decisions, the team must be empowered to filter the feedback, requests, and suggestions ([Larman, 2004][1]).
* Although tools are secondary to the team members themselves, they are still important.


[1]: # "Larman, C. (2004) Agile and Iterative Development: A Manager's Guide. Addison-Wesley. p. 27. ISBN 978-0-13-111155-4"
